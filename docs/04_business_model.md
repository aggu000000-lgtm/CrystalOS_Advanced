# Business Model

CrystalOS is built on a foundation of fairness and respect for the user. We believe that when you buy software, you should own it. Our business model reflects this core philosophy.

## Core Philosophy

CrystalOS is **paid software**.
*   **Not a subscription.**
*   **Not ad-supported.**
*   **Not data-harvesting.**

It is a **one-time purchase**. You pay a fair price, and you own it forever.

## Pricing Structure

| Edition | Price | Includes |
|---------|-------|----------|
| **Crystal Personal** | **$49** | OS + Store access |
| **Crystal Pro** | **$99** | OS + Store + IDE + Priority support |
| **Crystal Student** | **$19** | OS + Store (requires .edu email) |
| **Crystal Lifetime** | **$199** | All future updates forever |

## App Store Revenue

The Crystal Store operates on an industry-leading revenue split designed to attract and reward developers.

*   **Developer Share:** **90%** of app sales.
*   **CrystalOS Share:** **10%** (An industry low compared to the typical 15-30% taken by other platforms).
*   **One-Time Purchases Only:** No subscription requirements. Developers can offer paid updates if they choose to.

### Why 10%?
*   **Attracts Developers:** This competitive rate makes building for CrystalOS highly appealing.
*   **Sustainable Volume:** It provides enough revenue to sustain development at scale.
*   **Aligns Incentives:** When developers succeed, the platform succeeds.
*   **Fairness:** Developers do the hard work of creating apps; we simply provide the platform and infrastructure.

## No Other Revenue

We strictly adhere to a policy of transparency and respect for the user.

*   **No user data selling.**
*   **No ads in the OS.**
*   **No bundled crapware.**
*   **No "premium" subscriptions** for basic features.
*   **No telemetry phoning home** (Opt-in only, and fully anonymized).

## Financial Sustainability

Our model is designed for long-term growth and stability without compromising our values.

### Projections

*   **With 1 Million Users:**
    *   OS Sales: $49M (assuming an average $49 purchase)
    *   App Store Revenue: $2M/year (assuming the average user spends $20/year on apps, generating $2M in our 10% share)
    *   *Result: A sustainable team of 50-100 developers.*

*   **With 10 Million Users:**
    *   OS Sales: $490M
    *   App Store Revenue: $20M/year
    *   *Result: A serious company with a profound impact on the industry.*
