# CrystalOS

*"Boot in 3 seconds. Respond in milliseconds. Feel like forever."*
— CrystalOS Manifesto

CrystalOS is a visionary operating system built from the ground up to redefine how users and developers interact with their computers. It is not just software; it is a statement that computing can be beautiful, incredibly fast, and deeply respectful of the user.

By abandoning legacy bloat, intrusive ads, and data-harvesting practices, CrystalOS focuses entirely on providing a seamless, magical experience. From its custom Rust microkernel to its physics-based cursor and integrated development environment, every aspect of CrystalOS is designed to make you feel like your computer is alive.
